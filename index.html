About Me

Hi, I’m Aswin — a data analyst focusing on healthcare analytics, AI solutions, and accessibility-driven innovation. I specialize in turning hospital and operational data into clean, actionable insights.

I have a background in Mechanical Engineering and Python Data Science, and I’m currently studying Applied AI Solutions Development at George Brown College. My work combines Python, SQL, and modern BI tools to support evidence-based decision making.

My long-term mission is to use AI to improve accessibility and build tools that support people with disabilities — a passion shaped by my own lived experience.
